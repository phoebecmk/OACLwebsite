Our Services

End-to-end company
secretarial services for
global investors

From initial incorporation to annual compliance. Select any service below and add it directly to your cart.

Incorporation

New Company Formation

A complete Hong Kong private limited company setup, managed from name availability check through to Certificate of Incorporation. All Companies Registry correspondence handled; full first statutory documents produced.

Also available for offshore companies such as British Virgin Islands (BVI), Cayman Islands, and Singapore entities.

  • Company name availability check
  • Articles of Association preparation
  • CR1 / NNC1 filing with Companies Registry
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • First board resolutions & share certificates
  • Significant Controllers Register setup
  • AML/KYC due diligence (TCSP compliance)
HKD 6,000
Fixed fee

Government fees (CR HKD 1,720 + BR HKD 2350) are additional disbursements billed separately.

Turnaround: 3–7 business days from KYC completion

Annual Retainer

Annual Compliance Package

Comprehensive annual company secretary service covering all statutory maintenance obligations under the Hong Kong Companies Ordinance.

  • Company Secretary designation (statutory requirement)
  • Annual Return (NAR1) preparation & filing
  • Statutory register maintenance
  • Annual compliance calendar & proactive reminders
  • Board resolution templates
  • Companies Registry correspondence handling
HKD 4,105
Per year

Includes Company secretarial fee and documents preparation (HKD 4,000) and NAR1 filing (HKD 105) — billed together annually. Can be bundled with registered office. BR Fee of HKD 2,350 excluded from package.

Registered Address

Registered Office Address

Every HK company must maintain a registered office address in the territory. We provide a professional Sheung Wan address with mail handling included.

  • Registered address: Wing Lok Street, Sheung Wan
  • Receipt of statutory notices & official mail
  • Scanning & digital forwarding
  • Annual renewal handled automatically
HKD 4,000
Per year

Can be bundled with annual secretary retainer at a reduced combined rate. Contact us for bundle pricing.

Corporate Changes

Director & Shareholder Filings

Changes to directors, shareholders, or the significant controllers register require timely notification to the Companies Registry. We handle all forms, resolutions, and documentation.

  • Director appointment (ND2A) & resignation (ND2B)
  • Shareholder transfers & updated register
  • Significant Controllers Register (SCR) updates
  • Change of company name or address filings
  • Board resolutions for all changes
HKD 1,000
Per filing

Price is per filing. Add multiple to your cart if you need several changes processed simultaneously.

Banking Support

Bank Account Assistance

Opening a corporate bank account in Hong Kong has become increasingly rigorous for international applicants. We guide you through the entire process and liaise with relationship managers on your behalf.

  • Bank selection advisory
  • Application document preparation & KYC pack
  • Business background memorandum drafting
  • Liaison with bank relationship managers
  • Follow-up on pending applications
HKD 7,000
Fixed fee per bank account

Account approval is at the bank's sole discretion. This service covers the application and liaison process only, clients are responsible for any account opening fees charged by banks.
